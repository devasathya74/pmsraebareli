About Our School

Police Modern School – Building a Bright Future for Children

Located inside the secure campus of 25th Vahini PAC, Raebareli, Police Modern School is committed to providing high-quality primary education. We believe every child is unique and deserves an environment that fosters curiosity, discipline and joy. We build values, ethics and responsibility alongside education.
